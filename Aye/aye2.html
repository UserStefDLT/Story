Aye’s Backstory

The Aye of the Storm.

My name is Aye. Aye like in “Aye Aye Captain!” or in my case “Aye Aye Lil Cap!” which is how the crew of the ship usually answered when I relayed an order from the Captain, my mom. I was pretty good at it, because my mom and I shared a link. We could talk to each other's mind.

One day, when I was still too young to be out during a storm ♦. The sky warned us of a storm brewing and the ocean became quiet and calm. It seemed like a normal storm brewing, until it started to wisper and I was sent to a safer part of the ship, but when we move pass the storm I heard it was an eldritch storm. The kind of storm with creatures made of smoke and lighting, the kind of storm that carries screams and defening sounds that leads to madness for those who hear the horrors for too long, but personally I only heard wispers but too many to make sense of it.

The damage to the ship was too big and the navigator suggested a location which was a risk, but the ship wouldn’t have made it to another location.

Once we got there, Captain Mom (not her real name) left with some of the crew to get the repairs and supplies needed, but I never saw her again. Later in that week, some guards approached the ship. I didn’t tell them my real name, because my mom always told me to keep it a secret, and unless I'm getting married, no one really need to know it. They never asked my relationship with the captain, but the navigator said that they found me, I just agreed. I was later told that the captain of the ship was a pirate. I knew, but I acted surprised anyways.

I couldn’t get much information about what happened to my mom, the captain. When I was asked, I told them that I was well treated, which made them think that I was being groomed. I asked what happened to the captain, but they never answered me. I did manage to hear them while they were talking with eachother that the captain was turn into an Aboleth as a curse for her crimes or something.

Because I was not consider an adult and they thought I was being groomed, I was taken to a monastery. I didn’t like it much. They focus more in the innerself, but I couldn't care about it because that is not where my mother was.

So, I sneaked out very often. I was used to sneaking around because my mom never wanted me to fight. I managed to sneak into some libraries because I have heard that they had information, and I wanted to know what happened to my mom. But I had to bailed more times that I was able to get in.

Locks and Books

With time ♦, I made some friends who were also good at sneaking around, and one more than "friends"♥, but that is a story for another day.

With the help of my friends I learn some useful skills and how to avoid trouble. ♦

One taught me to pick locks, and why locks is what I should be looking for. Spoiler, because of locks I know where the more interesting books are hidden. Another taught that if I don't need something, I should leave it behind, after all, if I don't need it it is not worth the weight nor the hassle. But the advice that lead to the fondest memories came from the weirdest person I ever met. Because before him, I didn't know some people could change to any race of their choosing. He did try to keep it a secret, but I'm very observant.

The suggestion he gave me was to keep the lights out to not be found. While I was not able to read in the dark, He was, so, we stay in the library while he read for me. Do you remember that mind link thing, it is not just with my mom, but she told me to keep it just between us. I broke that promise. I don't even remember the moment I linked my mind with my his, it felt so natural, like if we have been doing it for a while. After that, reading at night was so easy, no light, no sound, just two weirdos reading books.

And yes, as time passed ♦, we did officially became more than friends ♥, but I keep that for myself because my mom was not there. I want my mother there with me when I get to go throught that in a more public way. (A cleric friend helped us, but we hide it from everyone else.)

Those were good times and as I mentioned, my fondest memories, speding all night with my "friend" opening the locks, getting into a book and then leaving the book just to get into a another book of different size, then leaving that book, for another book. Such variety within a single library. I couln't get enough, and while I was there, I tried all the books I could. Safe to say, we did found some interesting books when we gave ourself some time to actually read. One book in particular became my favorite. It was an old one behind one of those locks, that talked about distant things. It was hard to read at first, but it took my mind away for a while. One part of the book talked about wishing on a star. What a whimsical idea, wishing to the lights on the sky for knowledge or a miracle. I found the book so endearing that one special day my "not-just-a-friend" got it for me, so I could read it on my own.

Looking into the Stars

Not soon after, my "friend" disappeared, which stopped me from going to the library since turning the lights on at night was too risky. I only had one book to read, the one about the distant things, and making a wish. Without anything to lose, one night I made a wish... Funny thing about stars… did you know that they can talk back?

I don’t know about other stars, but this one answered me. The star answered in the same language I made the wish, a secret language I had with my mom. (she calle it deep speech)

The… let’s call her Star, because I can’t translate her name, taught me a couple of interesting things ♦. I wanted to repay for what she taught me and asked what could I do for her. She asked what is that which I want the most right now. My answer was knowledge and information, I want to know things. Mostly to find my mom, but it was also because I have accuaried a taste and hunger for knowledge. So, I didn't mention my mom which made me feel a little selfish, but my mom always told me that if I don't think about myself and do things for me at least from time to time, I will hate life and fail to be her dauther.

And thus, my Star asked me to tell her everything I know and learned, and in exchange I would be taught and given power and magic. My answer was too unfiltered by my own taste: "You didn't have to offer power and magic, you got me at 'tell me everything.'" But my Star replied. "Some laws are immutable I request what people desire the most and I have to give something in exchange, otherwise my little aye, there are spells that would collect that information you are sharing if I don't give you something in return." And so, I told her so many things that I have heard, read, and what I learned from my nights with my "friend". Safe to say, my star loved it, I even got some laughts from the jokes and the silly assumtions we make on this world. Sometimes my star even told me of what she sees because some how I was giving too much information and I was not getting enough experience to learn more spells.

After a while my Star gave me the ability to see in the dark, I call it Star's Sight, which is what allows me to continue my night research. I have to admit, the library was not what it used to be. It is not the same without my partner. I miss his smiles, I miss his voice in my mind reading me all night, I miss his arms around me, and I miss his breath in my cheek and neck, because I knew I was not alone. But I need to keep reading, to find at least some clue, and then when I'm on my quest to find my mother, I can also look for him.

One day I found a record of a pirate that turned into an Aboleth. Some believe she was a polimorphed or shapechanged into an Aboleth, but the record mentions a spell caster that worried that the pirate might have an enchantment, was casting dispell, but it happened that accidentaly dispell a True Polymorph spell on the pirate who in fact was an Aboleth. I guess that explains out secret language, and maybe it also explains why I can talk to people's minds.

It has been 5 years since the storm that changed my life ♦. I hope I can find where my mom is, but until then I will try to prepare myself and learn everything I can.

You and Aye
